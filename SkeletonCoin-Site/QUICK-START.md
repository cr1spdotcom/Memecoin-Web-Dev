# 🚀 Quick Start Guide - 3 Simple Steps

## Step 1: Publish (5 minutes)

### Easiest Method: Netlify
1. Go to **netlify.com** → Sign up (free)
2. Click **"Add new site"** → **"Deploy manually"**
3. **Drag your entire `meme-coin-website` folder** into the upload box
4. **Done!** Copy your URL (e.g., `https://something.netlify.app`)

---

## Step 2: Update URLs (2 minutes)

1. Open `index.html` → Press **Ctrl+F** → Find `yoursite.com`
2. Replace ALL `yoursite.com` with your real URL (from Step 1)
3. Do the same in `sitemap.xml` and `robots.txt`
4. **Re-upload** to Netlify (drag & drop again)

---

## Step 3: Submit to Google (5 minutes)

1. Go to **search.google.com/search-console**
2. **Add property** → Enter your URL → **Continue**
3. **Verify ownership:**
   - Copy the HTML tag Google gives you
   - Paste it in `index.html` (inside `<head>`)
   - Re-upload → Click **Verify**
4. Go to **Sitemaps** → Add `sitemap.xml` → **Submit**

---

## ✅ That's It!

Your site is now:
- ✅ **Live on the internet**
- ✅ **Submitted to Google**
- ✅ **Will appear in search results in 1-4 weeks**
